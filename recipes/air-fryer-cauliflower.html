Air Fryer Cauliflower

Description

Air Fryer Turmeric Cauliflower as seen on WholesomeMadeEasy.com

This simple recipe takes less than 10 minutes of prep and 15-20 minutes in your airfryer.

Light, colorful, and crispy, it's a great side for almost any meal. Spices can be adjusted to preference to create dozens of varieties with a little practice.

Additional Notes

Turmeric leaves yellow stains, beware!!

Spice blends like Penzey's Curry spice are a great option instead of the spices listed. Read their ingredients carefully and add salt as needed.

Spice and oil measurements are based on using a medium sized head of cauliflower. Adjust them up or down as needed.

When adding spices to the cauliflower, i like to shake 1/4-1/3 on at a time to avoid clumping and get a more even spread.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head of cauliflower
  • 1-2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp cumin

Steps

  1. Cut cauliflower into florets, put into large mixing bowl or preferably large bowl with lid.
  2. Pre-heat air fryer 400 Â°F
  3. Blend spices together in a separate bowl.
  4. Add spices to the cauliflower (see Notes), stirring or shaking to evenly distribute.
  5. Drizzle oil over cauliflower, stirring or shaking as you go just like the spices.
  6. Air fry 8 minutes, shake or stir, then 7 more minutes. Add additional time as needed/desired.
